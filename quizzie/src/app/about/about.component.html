Who are we?
Quizzie is an online gaming platform for quiz games and trivias. We also offer various kinds of personality tests as well as other fun games.
Our ambitions
Quizzie aims to provide its users with an easy-to-use quiz and trivia gaming platform.
Quiz points system
Quizzie points are calculated when you divide your corrent answers by the total answers of the quiz and multiply the result by the remaining minutes.
Custom quizzes
Quizzie offers the possibility to create a custom quiz. You can choose between 3 time frames for a quiz and between the number of questions. After creation, your quiz will be revealed, and eventually posted.
